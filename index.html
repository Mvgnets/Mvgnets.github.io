Welcome to Baker Nixon Welding Services Ltd

A forging die will last longer in the hands of experts, and no company has more expertise in repairs to forging dies than Pioneer. In fact, Baker Nixon has been repairing and improving forging dies for over 25 years.

Baker Nixon specializes in fabrication, flood and overlay welding repairs of forging dies and heavy plant repairs. We are also experts in on-site plant repairs. We provide a service required for a rapid and successful turnaround. Based in Middlesbrough in North East England, we are probably the most experienced flood welding operator in the UK. We can boast of a committed and motivated workforce, well known for the quality of our work and innovative ideas. We are always investigating new ways of tackling forging die and equipment repair problems so that we can provide our customers with even longer lifetimes and higher performance.
